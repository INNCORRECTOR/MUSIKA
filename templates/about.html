ABOUT MUSIK-A

MUSIK-A AS AN NGO: SERVICE AT ITS CORE

MUSIK-A operates under MUSIK-A Society, a registered NGO under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Regd. No. HOME/SRC-7309), headquartered at Yan Villa, NEZCC Road, near Dimapur Airport Gate, Diphupar, Nagaland.

As an initiative of the Society, MUSIK-A is not only an educational institute but also a platform that blends structured arts education with a strong commitment to community development. Guided by the motto “Share, educate and be enriched,” it works to spread knowledge across communities, nurture talent across generations, and enrich lives through music, arts, and culture.

This vision is reflected in its scholarships, community programs, and the learning environment it provides to every student.

OUR STORY & SCHOOL

WHERE HERITAGE MEETS
WORLD-CLASS MUSIC EDUCATION

MUSIK-A School stands among Nagaland's leading institutes for music and arts, delivering world-class training for learners from age 3 to advanced professional levels. Rooted in the rich musical heritage of the Naga people, MUSIK-A was built with one bold purpose: nurture talent, empower youth, and strengthen the region's creative future.

Since its founding on 6 May 2016, the institution has brought education, culture, performance, and community enrichment under one roof. Here, the legacy of Naga folk traditions meets globally recognized curricula, allowing first notes to grow into lifelong artistic journeys and meaningful careers.

Over the past decade, MUSIK-A has grown far beyond a conventional school. It has evolved into a living cultural movement that reflects the soul of Nagaland and the aspirations of its people.

MISSION & OPERATIONS

MUSIK-A is a registered society built on a simple belief: Share, educate, and be enriched. Based in Dimapur, Nagaland. MUSIK-A extends its mission across the entire nation, contributing significantly to the music, arts, culture, and education sectors.

Education & Training

Two music schools powered by 30+ professional musicians and educators. 25+ underprivileged students receive sponsored training every year.

Sound engineering programs since 2016 have supported 100+ churches across Northeast India.

Events & Cultural Work

100+ events organized since 2014, including festivals and concerts. A multidisciplinary team ensures impactful experiences.

Artist Development

Preparing artists to become industry-ready professionals with exposure to national and international platforms.

Artist Booking

Connecting events with performers from local talent to international acts, enriching the region’s cultural scene.

OUR IMPACT

Through music, education, and cultural initiatives, MUSIK-A continues to create meaningful opportunities for individuals and communities while maintaining a strong commitment to professionalism and excellence.

OUR FACULTY

THE FACULTY BEHIND MUSIK-A

Team MUSIK-A is made up of working artists and educators from Nagaland who are passionate about passing the craft of music to the next generation. They bring real stage, studio, and community experience into every class and rehearsal, keeping teaching grounded, practical, and inspiring.

Alobo Naga serves as Director and remains a North East musical reference point, guiding the school's artistic direction and outreach. The faculty and collaborators include artists such as Koko Savino, Thunglamo Ngullie, Kekhrie Ringa, Nono Savi, Jekhe, Bubu Sechu, and Kehepe Letro, with accompanying pianists and musicians such as Asenle Keppen, Cheteu, Lironi, Nash Longkumer, Augustine Eliem, and Abdon Mech—all contributing to recitals, productions, and the wider creative life of the school.
