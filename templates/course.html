Education & Training

A World-Class Curriculum

At the heart of MUSIK-A’s mission is a comprehensive, multi-disciplinary curriculum that spans classical traditions, contemporary genres, cultural heritage, and technical disciplines. Students aged three and above can access structured, progressive training designed to build genuine mastery — not just surface-level familiarity — with their chosen art form.

MUSIK-A’s affiliations with internationally recognized bodies such as the London College of Music (for Western Classical Music) and Rockschool UK (for Contemporary Music) ensure that students in Nagaland have access to the same rigorous standards as students in any major global city. This is a remarkable achievement for Northeast India, and one that MUSIK-A wears with pride.

♫Western Classical Music

London College of Music affiliated — internationally recognized grading and certification.

♪Contemporary Music

Rockschool UK affiliated — modern genres including rock, pop, and jazz.

✚Church Music

Specialized training for worship leaders, choir directors, and church musicians.

☉Indian Classical & Bollywood

Rich grounding in India’s classical traditions alongside popular contemporary styles.

❖Naga Folk Music Studies

Preserving and celebrating indigenous musical heritage as a formal discipline.

🎧Audio Engineering

Live Sound & Recording Arts — one of the few such professional programmes in Northeast India.

MUSIK-A remains among the very few institutes in Northeast India offering structured, professional Audio Engineering courses covering both Live Sound and Recording Arts — a significant contribution to the region’s creative industries.

Scholarships

Scholarships & Social Upliftment

One of the most profound expressions of MUSIK-A’s NGO identity is its long-standing scholarship programme. For over a decade, MUSIK-A has ensured that financial barriers do not prevent gifted and dedicated young people from accessing world-class music education. This is a commitment that goes beyond policy — it is embedded in the institution’s founding philosophy.

Who Benefits

  • ✓Children and youth from underprivileged families
  • ✓Students displaying exceptional talent and dedication
  • ✓Young artists needing financial support to pursue professional music

The scholarship programme has created ripple effects that extend far beyond the individual recipients. When a child from an underprivileged household receives music training, they gain not only a skill but a new sense of self-worth, discipline, and possibility. Many scholarship recipients have gone on to become teachers, performers, and audio engineers — individuals who now contribute to Nagaland’s cultural and economic life in meaningful ways.

Duration

Scholarships have been awarded continuously for over 10 years — making this one of Nagaland’s longest-running arts scholarship programmes.

This sustained commitment to social upliftment is what distinguishes MUSIK-A from a conventional arts institution and establishes it firmly as a community anchor — a place where opportunity is created, not just celebrated.
