Last updated: MAY 4

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve functionality, user experience, and performance.

2. Our Use of Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for:

  • Advertising
  • Retargeting
  • Marketing campaigns
  • User behaviour tracking

However, embedded media platforms (such as YouTube, Spotify, etc.) may place cookies when you interact with their content.

These cookies may be used for:

  • Playback functionality
  • Performance monitoring
  • Basic analytics by their platform

We do not control or access these cookies.

3. Types of Cookies That May Appear

  • Session cookies (via embedded players)
  • Performance cookies (set by third-party services)

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect media playback functionality.

5. Consent for Cookies

By using this website and interacting with third-party embedded content, you consent to the use of cookies according to their policies.

6. Updates to Cookies Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page. Related information may also appear in our Privacy Policy.
